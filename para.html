Paragliding in Pokhara: Adventure in the Sky

Paragliding over Pokhara Valley

Pokhara, Nepal, is renowned as one of the world's best paragliding destinations. Adventure enthusiasts soar above the stunning Phewa Lake, Annapurna mountain range, and lush green valleys for breathtaking views and unforgettable experiences.

Paragliding in Pokhara is suitable for beginners and experienced flyers alike. Tandem flights with certified instructors allow anyone to enjoy the thrill safely, making it a must-try activity for tourists visiting the city.
