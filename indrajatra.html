Indra Jatra: Festival of Kathmandu

Chariot Procession During Indra Jatra

Indra Jatra is one of the most vibrant and important festivals celebrated in Kathmandu, Nepal. It honors Lord Indra, the god of rain and king of heaven, and showcases traditional dances, chariot processions, and cultural rituals. The festival usually takes place in September.

The highlight of Indra Jatra is the procession of the chariots of Kumari, the living goddess, and other deities. Traditional dances such as Lakhe dance, masked dances, and bhairab performances attract locals and tourists alike, preserving the rich Newar cultural heritage.
