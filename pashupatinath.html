Pashupatinath Temple: The Sacred Hindu Shrine

Pashupatinath Temple

Pashupatinath Temple, located in Kathmandu, Nepal, is one of the most sacred Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is visited by thousands of pilgrims every year, especially during Maha Shivaratri. The temple complex is known for its beautiful pagoda-style architecture, riverbank ghats, and spiritual significance.

The temple is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and holds numerous religious rituals, ceremonies, and festivals. Devotees from all over Nepal and India come to seek blessings, perform cremation rituals on the Bagmati River, and witness traditional Newari architecture.
