Everest Base Camp: Gateway to the World's Roof

Everest Base Camp with Mount Everest in the Background

The Everest Base Camp (EBC) is one of the most famous trekking destinations in the world, located in the Khumbu region of Nepal at an altitude of around 5,364 meters (17,598 ft). It serves as the starting point for mountaineers attempting to summit the world’s highest peak, Mount Everest (8,848.86 meters).

The trek to Everest Base Camp offers stunning views of the Himalayas, including peaks like Amadablam, Lhotse, and Nuptse. Along the trail, trekkers experience Sherpa culture, ancient monasteries, and breathtaking landscapes.
