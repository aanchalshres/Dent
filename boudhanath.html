Boudhanath Stupa: A Symbol of Peace

The Majestic Boudhanath Stupa

The Boudhanath Stupa, located in the northeastern outskirts of Kathmandu, is one of the largest spherical stupas in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It serves as a central hub of Tibetan Buddhism in Nepal and attracts pilgrims and tourists alike.

The stupa’s massive white dome and all-seeing eyes of Buddha symbolize peace, unity, and awareness. Surrounding the stupa are prayer wheels, monasteries, and shops selling traditional Tibetan artifacts.
